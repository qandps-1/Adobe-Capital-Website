Our Team

Robert (Bob) Smith

Founder & Principal

Robert (Bob) Smith is the founder and Principal of Adobe Capital Partners, a diversified infrastructure company focused on energy services, data center development, industrial mining, advanced computing, aviation and financial services. He is also founder and CEO of Infrastructure X, a family office driving transformative innovation in energy technology, medical and bio advancements, defense technologies, AI, quantum computing, and financial technology. With 30 years as a public company executive, he blends strategic vision, legal expertise, and active investment acumen across private equity, venture capital, venture debt, hedge funds, and real estate development.

Richard Smith

Principal

Richard Smith is a seasoned financial executive with 30 years of leadership experience at top-tier global financial institutions. Throughout his career, he has held senior roles with an executive-level focus across operations, systems architecture, corporate finance, and mergers & acquisitions, consistently driving efficiency, scalability, and enterprise value.
